Welcome to Winter Wear Warehouse

I am convinced that there can be luxury in simplicity. I've treated the waistcoat as if it were a corset, so that it becomes the first layer in the process of putting clothes on the body. There is constant motion between layering and revealing. I believe that my clothes can give people a better image of themselves - that it can increase their feelings of confidence and happiness. The great thing about fashion is that it always looks forward. I like to be real. I don't like things to be staged or fussy.

I love a black wedding dress. I think there is something about luxury - it's not something people need, but it's what they want. It really pulls at their heart. Luxury will be always around, no matter what happens in the world. It's useless to send models out on the runway to cry. It should be natural, and that's how I like women to look. I like them to feel comfortable and look organic.

The key to my collections is sensuality. Fashion is to please your eye. Shapes and proportions are for your intellect. I'm used to always deciding everything myself. It's a blessing, but also a terrible defect. Fashion is made to become unfashionable. It should be natural, and that's how I like women to look. I like them to feel comfortable and look organic.
